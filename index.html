zuhano /'zoo-haw-no/ a. 1. Refers to an object, person, or liquid falling rapidly due to gravity. 2. Describes a sudden and drastic decline or collapse, such as a company's financial situation plummeting, someone's health worsening rapidly, or a politician's popularity dropping.
forint /'for-int/ n. 1. The basic monetary unit of Hungary. Name of forint comes from the city of Florence, where golden coins were minted from 1252 called fiorino d'oro.
